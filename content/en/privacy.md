SunBar helps you find bars, cafés and restaurants whose terraces are bathed in sunlight right now. This policy explains what little data the app needs to do that — and what it never does.

## What SunBar Does

SunBar uses the position of the sun — calculated locally from the current time and your location — to tell you which outdoor terraces nearby are likely to be sunny at this moment. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no behavioural tracking.

## Your Location

If you grant permission, SunBar reads your device's GPS or network position to centre the map near you. **Your coordinates are never sent to our servers.** They stay in your browser and are used only to:

- Fetch the list of nearby venues from our API
- Calculate the sun's altitude and azimuth using the open-source [SunCalc](https://github.com/mourner/suncalc) library

You can also search for any place manually or move the map freely without sharing your location.

## Search Queries

When you type a place name in the search box, the query is sent to [Nominatim](https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org) — the geocoding service run by the OpenStreetMap Foundation — to find matching locations. Nominatim's own [privacy policy](https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy) applies to those requests.

## The Interactive Map

The map is powered by **Esri / ArcGIS**. If you accept map cookies, your browser connects to Esri's CDN and tile servers (`cdn.arcgis.com`, `basemaps.arcgis.com`, `services.arcgisonline.com`). Esri may set cookies and collect technical usage data as described in [Esri's Privacy Statement](https://www.esri.com/en-us/privacy/privacy-statements/privacy-statement).

If you reject map cookies, the interactive map will not load, but all other features of SunBar work normally. You can change your choice at any time via the cookie banner.

## Cookies

SunBar itself sets **no analytics or tracking cookies**. The only optional cookies are those placed by the ArcGIS / Esri map service, and only when you explicitly accept them. Your preference is saved in your browser's `localStorage`.

## Community Contributions

When you submit a venue through the app, its name, type and coordinates become part of the public SunBar dataset. Please do not include personal information in venue submissions.

## Children

SunBar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
